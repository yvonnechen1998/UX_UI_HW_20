User Research

  • Conduct comprehensive user research to understand genre and location-based readers, as well as book club enthusiasts.
  • Use research findings as the basis for designing and developing the book.nook Community Platform.
  • Aim to connect users with niche book communities and create a vibrant reading community aligned with the project's hypothesis statement.

    • Problem Statement

    We believe that by creating a platform for book lovers, we can address the challenge of fostering a sense of community among like-minded readers, providing them with an enjoyable space to engage in discussion and enhance their overall reading experience.

    User Insight

    Busy professionals who are avid recreational readers need an engaging platform to connect with similar genre enthusiasts, because they want a fun place to build communities and make new book friends to have meaningful discussions and share recommendations.
